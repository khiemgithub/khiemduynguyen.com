Summertime = Internship Mode On

For Summer 2016, I was very fortunate to have landed an opportunity to work at GE Energy Connections as a UX Design Intern, within the Grid Solutions team. My main project revolves around the redesign of "e-terrasimulator Dispatcher Training Simulator (DTS)" - a software training system used by operators of electric power grids.

The field is unique, the product is complex (and old), and the need for a better User Experience/Interface that more closely aligns with modern standards is on the agenda. It is both a design challenge and opportunity, and I surely am enjoying every bit from this meaningful experience.

Updates Coming Soon

With the internship presently ongoing, I'm definitely keeping myself busy with an abundance of deliverables & learnings this summer. Do stay tuned for more updates once the project outcomes are ready to be showcased!
