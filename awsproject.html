  • January, 2016 - March, 2016

(Picture from TechCrunch)

A partnership with AWS

Amazon Web Services Internet of Things (AWS IoT) is a managed cloud platform that lets connected devices easily and securely interact with cloud applications as well as other devices. AWS IoT can support billions of devices and trillions of messages, and can process as well as route those messages to AWS endpoints and to other devices reliably and securely.

As part of the HCDE 517 class, our team has partnered with AWS to design and conduct a baseline usability study for the IoT platform. The goal? To measure the ease-of-use developers have when using AWS IoT to set up a development environment with various devices connected, and effectively transfer data between these devices and the environment itself.
